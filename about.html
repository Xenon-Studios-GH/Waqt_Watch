Heritage

Where Time Becomes Art

For over a century, WAQT has stood as a beacon of horological excellence. Our journey began in the heart of Geneva, where master watchmakers shared a singular vision: to curate timepieces that transcend mere functionality and become testaments to human artistry.

Today, we continue this tradition, bringing together the world's most prestigious manufactures under one roof—a sanctuary for those who understand that true luxury lies not in possession, but in appreciation.

Our Journey

Moments That Defined Us

Key milestones in our pursuit of horological perfection

1892

The Foundation

In a small Geneva atelier, our founding vision took shape—a commitment to curating the world's finest timepieces for discerning collectors.

1925

Global Expansion

Our first international boutiques opened in Paris, London, and New York, bringing European horological excellence to the world stage.

1968

Innovation Era

Pioneering modern authentication protocols and client services, setting new standards in the luxury watch industry.

1995

Asian Expansion

Bringing our curated collection to Asia with flagships in Tokyo, Hong Kong, and Singapore.

2024

Digital Renaissance

Embracing the digital age while preserving our commitment to personal service and authentic expertise.
